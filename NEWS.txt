oxcAAR v1.1 Flora (Release date: 2021-02-01)
=================

New features:
* enabled processing (reading, printing, plotting) of sequential calibration results from OxCal
* added ggplot versions of the plot of individual and multiple dates
* added Boundary, Phase, Sequence and wrap_in_boundary to OxCal convenience functions

Bug fixes:
* fixed parsing of sigma ranges
* fixed display of dates exceeding the calibration curve range
* fixed overprecise sigma range percentage reporting
